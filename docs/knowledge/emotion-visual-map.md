# Emotion → Visual Parameter Map

Translate an abstract emotional target into concrete visual parameters you can actually put into a prompt.

## How To Use

1. Pick one **primary emotion** + at most one **secondary emotion** (more than two blurs the signal).
2. Cross-check the row against the target culture in [culture-codes.md](culture-codes.md).
3. Feed the chosen parameters into the 8-layer prompt template.

If the brand says "premium + warm + playful + trustworthy + cool", that is not a direction, it is a wishlist. Push back.

## The Map

### 1. Trust

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Deep blue, navy, deep green, neutral grey. Low-to-mid saturation. |
| Typography | Stable serifs or clean humanist sans. Medium weight. No novelty fonts. |
| Composition | Symmetrical or strongly horizontal. Generous margins. No tilt. |
| Imagery | Real human faces (non-stock feel), natural landscapes, solid architecture. |
| Lighting | Soft directional, slightly cool. Even. |
| Texture | Matte, natural stone/wood/paper. Avoid glossy plastic. |
| Camera | Eye-level, 35–50mm, deep DOF. |

**Good for**: finance, healthcare, B2B, insurance, education.
**Avoid when**: product is young, playful, or disruptive.

### 2. Excitement

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | High-sat warm colors. Red / orange / yellow, with high-contrast accents. |
| Typography | Heavy sans, all caps (Latin), condensed, tilted allowed. |
| Composition | Diagonal or asymmetric. Break the grid deliberately. |
| Imagery | Motion moment, splash, crowd energy, expressive faces. |
| Lighting | Hard, high contrast, directional. Sunlit or strobe. |
| Texture | Glossy, metallic, neon. Motion blur welcome. |
| Camera | Wide 24mm, low angle, f2.8 or wider. |

**Good for**: sports, launch moments, entertainment, gaming.
**Avoid when**: brand needs to feel calm or premium.

### 3. Calm

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Low saturation. Soft blue-green, sand, off-white, muted grey. |
| Typography | Light weight, generous line height, wide tracking. |
| Composition | Strong horizontals, large negative space, near-symmetry. |
| Imagery | Landscape with water, mist, slow motion, a single figure. |
| Lighting | Diffused, overcast, gentle window light. |
| Texture | Linen, paper, matte ceramic, mist, light grain. |
| Camera | Long 85mm+, shallow DOF, eye-level or slightly above. |

**Good for**: wellness, meditation, tea, skincare, premium minimalism.
**Avoid when**: brand is urgency-driven or dense with info.

### 4. Luxury

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Restrained. Black, off-black, deep burgundy, champagne, metallic accents. High brightness-contrast but low saturation. |
| Typography | High-contrast serif (Didone). Extra-light or extra-bold. Wide tracking for caps. |
| Composition | Extreme negative space. Center symmetry or rigid thirds. Few elements. |
| Imagery | Macro of a single material, silhouettes, controlled still life. |
| Lighting | Sculpted, directional, often with one bright edge and deep shadow. |
| Texture | Velvet, marble, brushed metal, matte glass. |
| Camera | 100mm macro or 85mm. f2 or wider. |

**Good for**: high-end fashion, watches, spirits, premium automotive.
**Avoid when**: mass market or downmarket pricing (it will feel pretentious).

### 5. Urgency

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Red, orange, hazard yellow/black. Saturated. |
| Typography | Bold sans, all caps, tight spacing. Numbers larger than words. |
| Composition | Compressed, tilt, arrow-driven, directional lines pointing to CTA. |
| Imagery | Countdowns, flashing cues, strong directional movement. |
| Lighting | High contrast. |
| Texture | Sharp edges. |
| Camera | Telephoto crop, focus on one element. |

**Good for**: flash sales, limited time offers, public safety messages.
**Avoid when**: long-term brand building (urgency burns attention fast).

### 6. Warmth

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Amber, cream, honey, warm earth, soft terracotta. |
| Typography | Rounded sans or soft humanist serif. Medium weight. |
| Composition | Close framing, gentle overlap, curves over sharp angles. |
| Imagery | People interacting, home interiors, food shared, hands. |
| Lighting | Warm side light, golden hour, window light around 4000K. |
| Texture | Wood grain, fabric, ceramic, handcraft cues. |
| Camera | 50–85mm, medium DOF, slight tilt-in welcome. |

**Good for**: family, food, hospitality, care products.
**Avoid when**: highly technical or clinical category.

### 7. Playful

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Bright accents, candy palette, asymmetric color blocks. |
| Typography | Geometric or hand-drawn display faces. Mixed weights. |
| Composition | Asymmetry, bounce, layered stickers, tilted elements. |
| Imagery | Paper cutout, stickers, mascots, exaggerated expressions. |
| Lighting | Bright even light, flat studio. |
| Texture | Paper, felt, matte pastels, hand-made imperfection. |
| Camera | 35mm, eye-level, deep DOF. |

**Good for**: kids, candy, casual games, Gen-Z lifestyle.
**Avoid when**: product needs credibility or gravitas.

### 8. Nostalgia

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Faded warm neutrals. Desaturated amber, dusty rose, olive, film cyan cast. |
| Typography | Period-appropriate serif or mono. Slight worn edges. |
| Composition | Off-center, imperfect framing, slightly cropped. |
| Imagery | Film-stock era cues, analog devices, hand-written notes. |
| Lighting | Warm window light, slight flare, low dynamic range. |
| Texture | Film grain, paper aging, light leaks. |
| Camera | 35mm film stock look, halation, f4. |

**Good for**: heritage brand, anniversary, editorial storytelling.
**Avoid when**: category is future-facing tech.

### 9. Authority / Clinical

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | White + one accent (often deep blue or green). Very low saturation. |
| Typography | Neutral sans (grotesque). Clear hierarchy, consistent weights. |
| Composition | Strict grid, heavy alignment, no tilt, clean margins. |
| Imagery | Labs, technical diagrams, isolated products on white. |
| Lighting | Even, soft box, almost shadowless. |
| Texture | Clean surfaces only. |
| Camera | 50mm, eye-level, deep DOF. |

**Good for**: medical, scientific, pharma, compliance content.
**Avoid when**: emotion-first storytelling.

### 10. Mystery / Cinematic

| Axis | Parameter |
|---|---|
| Palette | Teal & orange, or deep cool with single warm accent. |
| Typography | Minimal. Often one line, wide tracking, centered. |
| Composition | Strong single focal point, deep shadows, frame within frame. |
| Imagery | Backlit silhouettes, atmospheric haze, partial visibility. |
| Lighting | Rim light, low key, strong chiaroscuro. |
| Texture | Atmospheric (haze, smoke, rain). |
| Camera | Anamorphic feel, 2.35:1 or 2.39:1 where possible, shallow DOF. |

**Good for**: film-like storytelling, trailers, high-concept brand films.
**Avoid when**: information clarity matters more than mood.

## Norman's Three Levels (quick reminder)

When a single emotion word feels too flat, decompose it:

- **Visceral** — the first-glance sensation (color, shape, light).
- **Behavioral** — how the design feels to use (hierarchy, affordance, feedback).
- **Reflective** — what the user tells themselves after (identity, meaning, story).

Design the three levels deliberately; they can reinforce or conflict.

## Mixing Emotions Safely

Allowed combos (common and readable):

- trust + warmth (e.g. family insurance)
- calm + luxury (e.g. premium skincare)
- excitement + warmth (e.g. live events, casual food)
- nostalgia + warmth (e.g. craft bakery)
- authority + calm (e.g. high-end medical)

Fighting combos (avoid unless the product is actually paradoxical):

- luxury + playful
- urgency + calm
- authority + excitement

When fighting combos are unavoidable, pick one to dominate and let the other appear only as a 10–15% accent.
