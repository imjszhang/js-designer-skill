# Brand Archetypes → Visual Direction

Based on 12 Jungian archetypes. Use this to turn a brand personality into a concrete visual direction you can feed to the Prompt Director.

## How To Use

1. Pick **one dominant archetype**; optionally **one supporting archetype** with ~20% influence.
2. Cross the archetype's visual parameters with the emotion target in [emotion-visual-map.md](emotion-visual-map.md).
3. Feed into the 8-layer prompt template.

Warning: if the brand team insists on 3+ archetypes, force them to rank. Unranked archetypes produce incoherent visuals.

## The Twelve

### 1. Hero

- **Promise**: strength, courage, overcoming.
- **Examples**: Nike, US Marines, FedEx.
- **Palette**: saturated red, black, steel grey. High contrast.
- **Typography**: heavy sans (Futura Bold, Oswald), all caps, tight leading.
- **Composition**: low-angle shot looking up, diagonal energy, centered subject with deep shadow.
- **Imagery**: peak-moment action, sweat, determination on faces.
- **Lighting**: hard rim light, high contrast, directional.
- **Keywords for prompt**: "heroic low angle", "hard directional light", "editorial sports photography", "saturated red accents".

### 2. Outlaw / Rebel

- **Promise**: break the rules, freedom, disruption.
- **Examples**: Harley-Davidson, Diesel, early MTV.
- **Palette**: black with neon/red/orange accents; intentionally clashing.
- **Typography**: grunge, distressed, mixed cases, handwritten.
- **Composition**: asymmetric, tilted, torn edges, collage.
- **Imagery**: unpolished reality, imperfection, raw texture.
- **Lighting**: neon, sodium, strobe, hard strobe shadow.
- **Keywords**: "grainy 400 iso", "neon sign reflection", "torn poster collage", "anti-glossy".

### 3. Magician

- **Promise**: transformation, wonder, invisible power.
- **Examples**: Disney, Apple product launches, Tesla.
- **Palette**: deep purple, midnight blue, gold accent; gradients.
- **Typography**: elegant sans with unexpected tracking; occasionally gothic.
- **Composition**: centered with symmetry, often framed or portal-like.
- **Imagery**: light beams, particles, levitation, reveal moments.
- **Lighting**: volumetric light, subtle glow, backlit haze.
- **Keywords**: "volumetric light rays", "iridescent highlights", "dark deep purple backdrop", "particles in air".

### 4. Everyman

- **Promise**: belonging, practicality, no-nonsense.
- **Examples**: IKEA, Target, Home Depot, Levi's.
- **Palette**: primary blues, friendly red, warm neutrals; moderate saturation.
- **Typography**: humanist sans (FF Meta, Verdana-style), readable at every size.
- **Composition**: grid-obedient, ordinary framing, eye-level.
- **Imagery**: real people doing real things, non-idealized bodies and homes.
- **Lighting**: window light, natural overcast, honest.
- **Keywords**: "documentary style", "natural window light", "real interior", "non-model faces".

### 5. Lover

- **Promise**: intimacy, sensuality, aesthetic beauty.
- **Examples**: Chanel Beauty, Godiva, Victoria's Secret.
- **Palette**: deep red, soft pink, warm gold, cream; desaturated in luxury direction.
- **Typography**: high-contrast serif (Didone), elegant italic, generous tracking.
- **Composition**: close framing, soft curves, centered or rule-of-thirds, negative space breathable.
- **Imagery**: skin, silk, hands touching, slow-motion moments.
- **Lighting**: soft window, warm golden edge, shallow DOF.
- **Keywords**: "85mm f1.4 shallow depth", "golden edge light", "silk textile", "soft pink warm tone".

### 6. Jester

- **Promise**: joy, play, don't take life too seriously.
- **Examples**: M&M's, Old Spice (modern), Ben & Jerry's.
- **Palette**: candy bright, intentionally clashing but balanced, color-blocked.
- **Typography**: rounded geometric, mixed weights, sometimes handwritten.
- **Composition**: asymmetric, bounce, overlap, cheeky negative space.
- **Imagery**: absurd scenes, exaggerated expressions, surreal mascots.
- **Lighting**: bright even studio or flash.
- **Keywords**: "studio flat lighting", "bright saturated primary colors", "playful asymmetric layout", "surreal product placement".

### 7. Caregiver

- **Promise**: protection, service, put others first.
- **Examples**: Johnson & Johnson, Dove, UNICEF, Volvo.
- **Palette**: soft blue, gentle green, cream, warm neutrals; low saturation.
- **Typography**: friendly humanist sans, medium weight, generous line height.
- **Composition**: protective framing (an arm around, a cup in hand), eye-level to slightly-above.
- **Imagery**: hands, care moments, quiet domestic scenes, multigenerational.
- **Lighting**: warm soft window, no harsh shadow.
- **Keywords**: "soft window light", "warm cream palette", "tender hand gesture", "quiet domestic scene".

### 8. Creator

- **Promise**: self-expression, original vision, aesthetic ambition.
- **Examples**: Adobe, Lego, Apple (creative pro side), Moleskine.
- **Palette**: neutral base + strong accent, often single brand color.
- **Typography**: editorial — a display serif plus a clean sans; strong hierarchy.
- **Composition**: grid with intentional breaks, white space as subject.
- **Imagery**: work in progress, tools in hand, studios, sketchbooks.
- **Lighting**: clean north-light, soft and neutral.
- **Keywords**: "north-light studio", "editorial magazine layout", "tool on clean desk", "generous negative space".

### 9. Innocent

- **Promise**: simple happiness, nostalgia, being good.
- **Examples**: Coca-Cola (classic), Dove, Aveeno.
- **Palette**: light pastels, sky blue, sun yellow, white.
- **Typography**: rounded sans or classic old-style serif.
- **Composition**: centered, simple, with sky/horizon visible.
- **Imagery**: childhood cues, nature, smiling faces without irony.
- **Lighting**: daylight, soft overcast.
- **Keywords**: "soft daylight", "pastel sky", "smiling non-ironic portrait", "simple centered composition".

### 10. Sage

- **Promise**: wisdom, truth, understanding.
- **Examples**: Google (original), BBC, Harvard, MIT.
- **Palette**: deep blue, forest green, warm cream, restrained.
- **Typography**: classical serif for headlines (Garamond, Caslon), neutral sans for body.
- **Composition**: book-page-like, strong hierarchy, generous margins.
- **Imagery**: books, observatory, archival photography, diagrams.
- **Lighting**: reading-lamp warm, or cool archival.
- **Keywords**: "library reading lamp", "classical serif typography", "archival photograph", "restrained composition".

### 11. Explorer

- **Promise**: freedom, discovery, the road.
- **Examples**: Patagonia, Land Rover, Jeep, The North Face.
- **Palette**: earth tones, khaki, forest green, sky blue; natural.
- **Typography**: rugged sans or slab serif, condensed is welcome.
- **Composition**: wide horizon, small human in landscape, path leading somewhere.
- **Imagery**: actual landscapes (not stock), worn gear, maps.
- **Lighting**: natural outdoor light, golden hour, overcast mountain.
- **Keywords**: "wide landscape with small figure", "worn gear texture", "golden hour mountain", "documentary travel photography".

### 12. Ruler

- **Promise**: control, order, authority, premium.
- **Examples**: Rolex, Mercedes-Benz, American Express, Hermès.
- **Palette**: black, deep burgundy, gold, cream; very restrained.
- **Typography**: high-contrast serif or refined sans, wide tracking, small sizes.
- **Composition**: rigorous symmetry or golden section, heavy negative space.
- **Imagery**: single object, authority architecture, heritage material.
- **Lighting**: sculpted single source, deep shadow.
- **Keywords**: "single directional light with deep shadow", "marble or velvet surface", "rigorous symmetry", "gold accent on black".

## Quick Pairing Table (archetype × emotion)

| Archetype | Natural emotion fit | Forbidden emotion mix |
|---|---|---|
| Hero | excitement, urgency | calm, nostalgia |
| Outlaw | excitement, rebellion | luxury, authority |
| Magician | mystery, wonder | everyman, clinical |
| Everyman | warmth, trust | luxury, mystery |
| Lover | warmth, luxury, nostalgia | clinical, urgency |
| Jester | playful, excitement | luxury, authority |
| Caregiver | warmth, trust | urgency, rebellion |
| Creator | originality, calm | urgency |
| Innocent | calm, warmth | urgency, luxury-dark |
| Sage | trust, authority | playful |
| Explorer | freedom, warmth | luxury-polished |
| Ruler | luxury, authority | playful, everyman |

## Archetype Drift Warning

Every 3–5 years brands drift toward the archetype that is "in style" (recently: Creator + Everyman minimalism). Ask the brand whether their archetype is an actual strategic choice or a market-follower move. If it's the latter, push for differentiation before locking the visual direction.
